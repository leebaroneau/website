# Ideal Role

## Target titles

- Frontend Developer
- UX Engineer
- Product Designer with strong implementation ownership

## What I want more of

- Shipping real product and ecommerce experiences
- Working closely with senior developers and strong design or product peers
- Accessibility, performance and measurable user outcomes
- Teams that use both user feedback and metrics to improve the work

## Environment

- Established team rather than a completely undefined solo role
- Remote-friendly or Sydney-based
- High-craft product culture with room to learn
- Clear ownership, healthy collaboration and practical mentorship
