# Overview

iDarts Zen was a digital project from the DRKZEN agency period. The engagement covered both website development and social media support for a niche sports and lifestyle brand — one of several projects that required working across the full digital presence rather than a single deliverable.

# What I did

- Designed and delivered website work for the brand
- Supported social media content, visuals and production
- Maintained brand consistency across web and social outputs

# Outcome

A smaller agency engagement that demonstrates flexibility across web and digital marketing deliverables within the same project scope. It reflects the kind of multi-discipline work that was typical of the DRKZEN studio period, where design, implementation and content often overlapped in a single engagement.
