# Overview

Clique Threads was a full-scope design engagement covering brand identity, system design, interface and implementation. The brief was broader than most — it required owning everything from the logo through to the live site.

# What I did

- Led branding direction and logo development
- Defined the visual styleguide and design system
- Designed the site UI
- Provided implementation support through to launch

# Outcome

One of the strongest examples of end-to-end ownership in the portfolio. Demonstrates that I can own a brand from concept through to working site without handing off between design and implementation.
